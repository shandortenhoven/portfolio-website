Case study

Kubo

A calm identity vault concept designed to help people keep important documents organized and easy to find — especially when time is tight.

Why this exists

When documents expire, travel is coming up, or admin tasks stack up, people need a system that reduces cognitive load. Kubo explores a clean, reassuring interface with clear categorization and fast retrieval.

Design principles

  • Calm first: soft spacing, clear hierarchy, minimal noise.
  • Find fast: search + structured categories.
  • Reduce stress: “expiring soon” surfaced clearly, with simple actions.

Outcome

Kubo demonstrates a minimalist, trustworthy UI direction for document management — designed to feel lightweight, friendly, and easy to navigate.
