About

I’m a UX/UI designer who likes calm layouts, strong hierarchy, and products that feel obvious to use. My background in hospitality management trained me to stay structured under pressure, and to design with real people in mind.

In practice, that means I’m comfortable owning the messy middle: turning research into flows, flows into screens, and screens into something you can test, iterate, and ship. I’m especially drawn to products where clarity and trust matter: onboarding, payments, account areas, and anything people rely on when they’re stressed or in a hurry.
