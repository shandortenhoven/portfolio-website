Case study

Expert

A responsive mentorship platform concept where users can quickly find credible experts, book a session in one click, and leave with actionable next steps.

Project overview

  • Goal: Make mentorship feel fast, trustworthy, and easy to book.
  • Focus: Trust signals, clean discovery, clear CTAs, and strong hierarchy.
  • Deliverables: Personas, user flows, wireframes, hi-fi UI, usability testing insights.

My role

  • UX research synthesis
  • Information architecture
  • User flows & wireframes
  • UI design & iterations
  • Usability testing analysis

Problem

People want quick, practical advice — but struggle to judge credibility, compare experts, and understand what they’re paying for. The experience needs to feel calm, trustworthy, and lightweight (not like a spammy social network).

Outcome

The final concept emphasizes credibility and speed: clear expert cards, consistent UI patterns, and a booking flow that feels direct. Post-session, users can leave feedback and get structured next-step suggestions — keeping momentum after the call.
